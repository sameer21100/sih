EduVizion: Bridging
Data to Transform Education

School dropouts are a pressing issue in education systems worldwide, representing a significant challenge to both individuals and society as a whole. High dropout rates can have far-reaching social and economic consequences, affecting not only the individuals who leave school prematurely but also their communities and countries.
Early Warning Systems: Develop prototypes for early warning systems to identify at-risk students promptly.
Personalized Learning: Create prototypes for personalized learning platforms to cater to individual student needs.
Mentoring and Support Networks: Design prototypes for mentoring programs and support networks to connect students with mentors and peer groups.
Career Exploration: Develop prototypes to showcase career opportunities and motivate students to stay in school.
Parental Involvement: Create prototypes of parent engagement platforms to improve communication between schools and parents.
